About Me.

I’m not just here to make things pretty.
I design to solve real problems and create experiences that drive business goals and engage users.

My degree in Graphic Information Technology gave me a solid foundation in visual communication and user-centered design principles. Since then, I've committed myself to creating purposeful designs that tell stories and deliver real impact.

When I’m not designing, you’ll find me editing videos, writing songs and poems, or experimenting in the kitchen. Growing up in a creative Jamaican home, the spirit of community and curiosity shapes everything I do.
